Pasta

Description

A delicious dish of pasta with tomato sauce and meatballs

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make round meatball
  2. Fry them a bit
  3. Heat up the sauce
  4. Add the meatballs in
  5. Boil the pasta
  6. Add pasta to the sauce
  7. Grate some cheese on it
