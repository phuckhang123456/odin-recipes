Fried Chicken

Description

A delicious dish of pasta with tomato sauce and meatballs

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make egg wash
  2. Deep the chicken into the egg wash
  3. Role the chicken over breadcrumbs
  4. Fry with high heat
  5. Serve the chicken
